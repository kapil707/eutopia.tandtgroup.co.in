NUMEROUS PLANTS WITH RESTORATIVE PROPERTIES

At Eutopia, we took inspiration from science and planted more than 1000 plants with therapeutic value. A vast majority of these plants were shortlisted based on their high phytoncide release levels. These phytoncides are airborne chemicals with antimicrobial properties that plants emit to protect themselves from harmful pathogens. However, scientists have also found a positive

correlation between high phytoncide intake and strong immune system. Therefore, at Eutopia you are not only offered proximity to nature but also a space to enhance your body’s defenses, especially today, when we need stronger immune systems than ever. Now you have ‘tree therapy’ at your disposal at all times – a rarity for most urban dwellers.
